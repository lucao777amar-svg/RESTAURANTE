Our Story

Born in a small diner, built on big flavor

Red Ember started as a tiny roadside stand on Route 66, when Earl Ember decided the world needed a better burger. Armed with a cast-iron grill, a jar of secret spices, and endless stubbornness, he built something that outlasted trends, chains, and time itself.

Today, we operate across 6 cities — still using Earl's original recipe, still grilling over flame, still believing that food made with pride tastes different.
